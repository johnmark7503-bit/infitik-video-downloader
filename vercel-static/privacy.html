Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information InfiTik Video Downloader processes, why it is used, and the choices available to visitors.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the InfiTik Video Downloader website, its online downloader, related legal pages, and analytics features. By using the website, you acknowledge the practices described here.

Privacy-first processing: InfiTik is designed to process public media links only. The website does not require an account and does not intentionally store downloaded media files.

2. Information we process

Depending on how the website is used, we may process:

  • The public social-media URL submitted to the downloader.
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring page, date and time, and general interaction data.
  • Analytics events such as a download-button click, platform category, result status, and requested format.
  • Cookie or similar-technology identifiers where analytics or advertising services are enabled.

Do not submit private links, credentials, personal documents, or URLs containing sensitive information.

3. Submitted links and download processing

A submitted public URL is sent to a third-party video-processing API so that available media formats can be identified. The provider may process the URL and technical request data under its own terms and privacy practices. InfiTik does not intentionally save the submitted full URL in Firebase Analytics or Firestore, and it does not host the resulting media file.

4. Log files

Like most websites and hosting platforms, InfiTik and its infrastructure providers may use log files. Log data may include IP addresses, browser and device details, internet service provider, referring and exit pages, timestamps, request paths, response codes, and diagnostic information. Logs are used for security, abuse prevention, debugging, service reliability, traffic analysis, and aggregate reporting. Log information is not intended to directly identify a visitor.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

InfiTik may use first-party or third-party cookies, local storage, pixels, and similar technologies for essential operation, preferences, analytics, fraud prevention, advertising measurement, and—where permitted—personalized advertising. Browser settings can be used to block or delete cookies, but some features may not operate correctly afterward.

Where applicable law requires consent for non-essential cookies or local storage, visitors should be given an appropriate consent choice before those technologies are activated.

6. Google AdSense and advertising cookies

If Google AdSense or another Google advertising service is enabled, third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a visitor's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to show personalized ads based on visits to InfiTik and other sites.

Visitors may manage or opt out of personalized Google advertising through Google Ads Settings. Additional information is available in How Google uses information from partner sites and apps. Other advertising vendors or networks may use their own cookies and offer separate opt-out mechanisms.

7. Firebase Analytics and Firestore

If Firebase is configured, Google Firebase may process analytics and event data. InfiTik's download-event helper is designed to record the platform category, event status, format, page path, and timestamp—not the complete submitted video URL. Firestore may store these limited event records for product analytics and reliability monitoring.

8. How information is used

  • Provide and improve the downloader.
  • Measure performance and understand aggregate usage.
  • Detect abuse, automated traffic, fraud, and security incidents.
  • Diagnose errors and maintain service availability.
  • Display, measure, and improve advertising where enabled.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce site terms.

9. Third-party services

The website may rely on hosting, analytics, database, advertising, and media-processing providers. These services operate under their own privacy policies. InfiTik is not responsible for the privacy practices of external platforms or the destination pages reached through third-party links.

10. Retention and security

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and security. Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used, but no internet transmission or electronic storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

11. Your choices and privacy rights

Depending on location, visitors may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing, and may have the right to withdraw consent. Visitors can also manage cookies through browser controls and advertising preferences through the relevant provider settings. Requests can be submitted using the contact method below.

12. Children's privacy

InfiTik is not directed to children under 13, or the minimum age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such information is identified, reasonable steps will be taken to remove it.

13. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the website, providers, or legal requirements change. The latest version will be published on this page with a revised update date.

14. Contact

Privacy questions or requests may be sent through the developer contact method shown on the official InfiTik Google Play listing.
